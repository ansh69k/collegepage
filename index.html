ABOUT REC

Rajalakshmi Engineering College (REC), situated in Chennai, is renowned for its commitment to academic excellence and all-round development. The college has a spacious campus equipped with modern facilities and cutting-edge infrastructure, offering a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology. REC fosters a dynamic learning atmosphere, facilitated by experienced faculty who nurture students to excel in their chosen fields. The college is dedicated to promoting innovation and research, providing ample opportunities for students to engage in projects and industry collaborations. Additionally, REC emphasizes co-curricular and extracurricular activities, ensuring students receive a well-rounded education that prepares them for the challenges of the professional world. Rajalakshmi Engineering College (REC) in Chennai was established in 1997 and has been a leader in engineering education. As an affiliate of Anna University Chennai and approved by AICTE, REC is known for providing quality education and promoting innovation. The college offers bachelor's and master's programs across different fields, giving students a well-rounded learning experience. REC also takes pride in its diverse certification offerings, which help students gain globally recognized credentials. REC offers a variety of affordable fees to cater to students from different financial backgrounds. The college is dedicated to the well-rounded growth of its students, as evident from its impressive job placement record. With an average salary package that is competitive with industry norms, REC ensures its graduates enter the professional world feeling confident and capable. The placement data demonstrates REC's unwavering commitment to providing its students ample opportunities to launch their careers and fulfill their aspirations.
