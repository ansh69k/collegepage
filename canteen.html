CANTEEN

The college is equipped with a fully functional vegetarian canteen to serve hygienically prepared food to the staff and students. A subsidized mess is also operative in the campus. A separate mess for boys has been provided.

The mess is spacious with a dining hall and kitchen. Cafeteria serves hygienically prepared vegetarian food to the staff and students. A subsidized mess for boys has been provided.

The Institute has an excellent cafeteria with a seating capacity for more than 200 persons. It has a well-planned hygienic kitchen run by a reputed caterer at administered prices. Multi-cuisine lunch and snacks are being served in the cafeteria. They provide veg and non-veg menu simultaneously on all the days. A fast food restaurant-cum-convenience store is also there.

Canteens and cafeterias are one of the important parts of student life where you can find most of students chatting and gossiping on different topics. It plays a vital role in removing tensions and refreshing students. The college canteen is fairly new and provides enough and delicious food at affordable rates. It provides all types of nutritious and hygienic eatables and beverages to cater to the students, staff and visitors.

A wide variety of lunch, break-fast eateries with snacks are available in the college with hygienic environment. Adequate measures have been taken keeping student's health in mind providing safe drinking water in the college canteen. College canteen provides delicious and hygienic food articles at reasonable price. South indian, North indian, Chinese food , both vegetarian and non vegetarian are available
